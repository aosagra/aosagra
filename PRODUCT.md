# aosagra.com

The public digital home of the Agra Orthopaedic Society for members, surgeons, trainees, collaborators, patients, and the wider Agra community.

## Brand

Academic, dignified, current, and locally rooted. The experience should communicate education, ethics, and excellence without looking like a commercial hospital or generic healthcare template.

## Principles

1. AOS before aesthetics: the society and its work are always the subject.
2. Academic clarity: calendars, events, teaching, publications, and notices are easy to find.
3. Current activity and enduring legacy both matter.
4. Public awareness content is a civic service, not a marketing funnel.
5. Incomplete source material is labelled honestly instead of fabricated.

## Accessibility

Target WCAG 2.2 AA and support keyboard, touch, reduced-motion, and narrow-screen use.
